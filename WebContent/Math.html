Math

In math, we learned and used Wolfram's Mathematica to aid us in math modeling. Math modeling uses math techniques in the classroom and applies them to real world problems, taking into account real word conditions and challenges. The first file attached below is the gas station problem presentation that describes how my group of Anrew Yang, Neil Kale, and I modeled the conditions in which traveling farther for cheaper gas is worth the trouble. The second document is my team of Darshan Krishnaswamy, Akhi Yarlagadda, Will Hung, and I's submission to HiMCM, a 36 hour math modeling competition. We attempted to model modern power consumption and used that to predict the future.
